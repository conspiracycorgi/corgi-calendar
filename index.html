How to Use This Calendar

The Corgi Consciousness Calendar tracks lunar cycles to predict your corgi's mood and energy levels. Each day shows:

  • Gregorian Date (G#): Standard calendar date
  • Lunar Date (L#): Day in the 28-day lunar cycle
  • Mood: Your corgi's predicted mood based on lunar phase
  • Activity: Suggested activities that match your corgi's energy level
  • Moon Phase: Current phase of the moon

Special moon days (New Moon, First Quarter, Full Moon, Last Quarter) are highlighted with an orange border.

The lunar cycle influences your corgi's energy, mood, and behavior patterns in fascinating ways.

New Moon:

First Quarter:

About the Corgi Consciousness Calendar

The Corgi Consciousness Calendar is a unique tool designed to help corgi owners understand and anticipate their pet's moods and energy levels based on lunar cycles. Just as the moon affects tides and natural rhythms, it can influence your corgi's behavior patterns.

Use this calendar to plan activities that align with your corgi's natural energy levels, creating a more harmonious relationship and enhancing your corgi's well-being.
